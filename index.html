What Booked actually fixes.

What does Booked do?

Booked builds discoverability infrastructure for fashion creatives. It audits visibility, portfolio structure, trust signals, contact paths, and AI-readable context so photographers, stylists, models, creative directors, and boutique agencies become easier to find, understand, trust, and book.

What does a Booked portfolio audit include?

A Booked portfolio audit reviews image sequencing, casting readability, art-direction value, brand fit, credits, contact friction, search readability, and AI-discoverability signals. The goal is to clarify what to keep, what to cut, and what infrastructure needs to be built around the work.

How do I get more bookings as a fashion photographer?

More bookings usually require more than stronger images. A fashion photographer needs a clear portfolio sequence, searchable project context, visible credits, a direct contact path, and a digital presence that makes their style, market, and commercial value easy to understand quickly.

What does discoverability mean for a fashion creative?

Discoverability means whether the right people and systems can find, understand, trust, and recommend your work. For a fashion creative, this includes Google visibility, portfolio structure, credits, captions, filenames, metadata, social consistency, and a professional booking path.

How does Booked work?

Booked starts with diagnosis. It reads your portfolio, search presence, trust signals, and industry readability before recommending a fix. The outcome may be a visibility scan, portfolio audit, AI readability layer, machine-readable portfolio build, roster intelligence system for agencies, or a Legacy complete infrastructure build.

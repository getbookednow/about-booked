Revisions

All project services include a defined number of revision rounds:

  • Structure (website build): two revision rounds included. A third round is billable at R1,500 per round.
  • Landing page: one revision round included. Additional rounds at R800 per round.
  • Bio rewrite: one revision included. Additional revisions at R500 each.

A revision is defined as consolidated feedback on a delivered draft, submitted in a single message or document. Feedback submitted in multiple rounds after a revision has been actioned is treated as an additional revision. Revisions that expand the original scope are treated as new work and quoted separately.

Deposits are non-refundable in all circumstances except where Booked cancels a project due to circumstances within its control, in which case the full deposit is returned.
